# The Two Man Rules & Format

## Overview

- Format: 2-Man Team Tournament
- Teams: 18
- Structure: Pod Play -> Playoffs -> Champion
- Season: May 1 - October 1

## Timeline

- May - June: Pod Match 1 & 2
- July: Quarterfinals
- August: Semifinals
- September: Championship

## Tournament Structure

- 18 teams are divided into 6 pods of 3 teams
- Each team plays 2 pod matches
- 6 pod winners + 2 wild cards = 8-team playoff

## Match Format

- 2-Man Net Better-Ball Match Play
- Matches are 18 holes
- Each player uses 90% of their Course Handicap
- Course Handicap is based on:
  - Tee played
  - Course rating
  - Slope rating

## Handicap Rules

- Lowest handicap player plays off 0
- Other players receive strokes based on the difference
- Strokes are applied per scorecard handicap allocation

## Stroke Limitation

- Maximum of 1 stroke per hole per player

## Pod Scoring System

- Each hole is worth:
  - Win = 1 point
  - Tie = 0.5 points (each team)
  - Loss = 0 points
- Teams accumulate points across both pod matches

## Pod Standings

Teams are ranked by:

1. Match Record
2. Total Hole Points
3. Total Holes Won
4. Lowest Cumulative Net Better-Ball Score
5. Coin flip

### Notes

- A team going 2-0 automatically wins the pod
- If all teams go 1-1, tiebreakers determine standings

## Wild Card Qualification

- The top 2 non-pod winners advance to playoffs

Wild cards are ranked by:

1. Match Record
2. Total Hole Points
3. Total Holes Won
4. Net Better-Ball Score
5. Coin flip

## Playoff Seeding

- Seeds 1-6 (Pod Winners) ranked by:
  1. Match Record
  2. Total Hole Points
  3. Total Holes Won
  4. Net Better-Ball Score
  5. Coin flip
- Seeds 7-8 (Wild Cards) ranked using wild card criteria

### Bracket

- 1 vs 8
- 2 vs 7
- 3 vs 6
- 4 vs 5
- In the bracket, the higher seed gets to pick what course the match is played at

## Playoff Match Tiebreaker

If tied after 18 holes:

1. Sudden death playoff (if feasible)
2. Otherwise: Net scorecard playoff (holes 18 backward)
3. Coin flip

## Course & Tees

- Matches played at a mutually agreed course
- Course must be:
  - Regulation
  - Rated with slope
- Players may play different tees
- Handicaps must reflect tees played

## Scheduling Rules

- One match per month
- Must be completed within the assigned month
- Teams are responsible for scheduling
- Comish may assign forfeits if not completed

## Forfeit Policy

- Win awarded to opponent
- Winning team receives:
  - 12 hole points
  - +6 holes won
- No best-ball score recorded

## Score Submission

After each match, teams must submit:

- Match result
- Total hole points
- Total holes won
- Net better-ball score
- Scorecard (photo or verified)
- Must post match score to GHIN

## Key Principles of the Format

- Winning matches matters first
- Every hole matters
- Scoring is used only as a final tiebreaker
- Handicap system ensures fairness across 3-25 handicaps
- 90% handicap reduces volatility and improves competitive balance
